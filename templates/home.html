The Carbon Emission calculation from vehicles determines
the CO2 emissions of a onroad vehicle that arise during
a journey. The calculation is made per vehicle and not per
person. It not only takes into account the direct emissions
arising from fuel combustion but also so-called grey emissions.
These arise from the production of the vehicle, the provision
of road infrastructure and the extraction, transportation and
processing of the crude oil.
